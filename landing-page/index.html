Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

Hold Alt key, speak naturally, release to transcribe. Text appears wherever your cursor is. It's that simple.

Does it need internet?

No! VoiceLite runs 100% offline. The AI models are included. Your voice data never leaves your computer.

What's the difference between models?

Tiny (77MB): Instant, good accuracy. Small (488MB): Fast, great accuracy. Large (3GB): Best accuracy, slower. You get all models with any license.

Can I try it first?

Yes! Download includes a 14-day free trial with all features. No credit card required.

System requirements?

Windows 10/11, 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended for Large model), 5GB disk space for all models.
